ABOUT SHOPEE

Shopee is an electronic commerce website headquartered in Singapore owned by Sea Limited (formerly known as Garena), which was founded in 2009 by Forrest Li.Shopee first launched in Singapore in 2015, and has since expanded its reach to Malaysia, Thailand, Taiwan, Indonesia, Vietnam and the Philippines.

In 2015, Shopee was first launched in Singapore. Shopee was first launched as a consumer to consumer (C2C) marketplace. However, now they have switched to a hybrid C2C and business to consumer (B2C) model since launching Shopee Mall, which is an online shop platform for well-known brands. In 2017, the platform recorded 80 million app downloads with more than four million sellers and more than 180 million active products. In the fourth quarter of 2017, Shopee reported a gross trading value (GMV) of US$1.6 billion, up 206 percent from the previous year. Shopee had a total GMV value in 2018 of US$2.7 billion, up 153 percent from 2017.

In Malaysia, Shopee became the 3rd most visited e-commerce portal in Q4 2017, replacing Lelong and surpassing Lazada as the best app on Google Play and iOS App store.

Likewise, among consumers in Indonesia, a survey conducted in December 2017 by TheAsianParent revealed that Shopee was the first choice shopping platform for mothers in Indonesia (73%), followed by Tokopedia (54%), Lazada (51%), and Instagram (50%).

In April 2020, Shopee Indonesia began promoting sales of ready-to-eat food on the Shopee platform to become the third player competing with Gofood and GrabFood. This program, called ShopeeFOOD, has recruited more than 500 food sellers in the Jakarta area.

AFFILIATE

Shopee Affiliates are Affiliates who register and have Subscribers/Followers/Friends with a total of under 2,000 people on their social media accounts.

Shopee Influencers are Affiliates who register and have more than 2,000 Subscribers/Followers/Friends on their social media accounts.

Shopee Partners are Affiliates who have a minimum number of Subscribers/Followers/Friends. 2,000 people on his social media accounts and reached 50 orders min. 1 (one) time in the last 3 (three) months.

The Shopee Partners Program is an agreement between the Shopee Team & KOL which is valid for 6 (six) months and is renewed automatically, except that Shopee Partners must fill out the Shopee Affiliates Partner Renewal form before the agreement period is renewed. Learn more about Shopee Partners.

The advantage of Shopee Affiliate is that you get a commission of 5% from shops that are promoted, and 1% from shops that are not promoted.

The commission earned can be calculated by:
Calculation = % Commission × Price
Commission is calculated upon completion of a valid transaction of Star Seller/Star+/Mall products on the Shopee platform.
